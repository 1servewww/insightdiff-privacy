Privacy Policy for InsightDiff

Last updated: 2026-04-21

This Chrome extension is designed to compare text and JSON. All comparison is performed locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your content to any server.

Information we process

The extension may process the content you paste or load into the extension, such as text or JSON data, in order to generate diffs and formatting results.

How we use information

We use the content only to provide the extension’s core features, such as diff comparison, formatting, and summary display.

Data sharing

We do not share user data with third parties.

Data retention

We do not retain user data.

Permissions

The extension only requests permissions needed for its functionality.
